## What it does
Shows stakers exactly how much yield they'll earn after the validator's cut. Interactive sliders/inputs — updates in real-time as user adjusts values.

## Inputs (all adjustable)
1. Your stake amount (TAO) — number input, default 10
2. Validator's total stake (TAO) — number input, default 10,000
3. Validator take % — slider 0-50%, default 18% (the Bittensor standard)
4. Subnet emission share % — slider 0-10%, default 2% (represents subnet's share of daily emissions)
5. TAO price (USD) — number input, default pulled from /api/price (fall back to 300 if unavailable)

## Outputs (calculated, live as inputs change)
Show all of these clearly:
- Your share of delegated stake (%)
- Daily TAO earned (est.)
- Monthly TAO earned (est.)
- Annual TAO earned (est.)
- Annual APY % (est.)
- Daily USD value (est.) — using TAO price input
- Annual USD value (est.)

## Math (implement exactly)
Emission formula from official Bittensor docs:
- Total daily TAO emitted: 3,600 TAO
- Subnet daily emissions = 3,600 × (subnet_emission_share / 100)
- Validator share of subnet = subnet_daily × 0.41 (validators get 41%)
- Delegated portion = validator_share × (delegated_stake / total_validator_stake)
  where delegated_stake = total_validator_stake - validator_own_stake (assume validator owns 20% by default)
- After validator take: staker_pool = delegated_portion × (1 - validator_take/100)
- Your share: staker_pool × (your_stake / total_delegated_stake)

Add a note: "Calculations are estimates based on current emission parameters. Actual yield varies with network conditions."
