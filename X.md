## Tips

- Post link as a **reply** to the main tweet (X suppresses reach on tweets with links)
- Tag 3–5 accounts per tweet max — tagging too many looks spammy
- Rotate which accounts you tag to keep it natural
- Engage back within 30 min of posting for algo boost
